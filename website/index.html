Our free of cost 'AI Sports Coach' web-app, which uses ML algorithms built with TensorFlow and other Python libraries to train users in their chosen sports.

It processes live video to provide real-time analysis and immediate feedback through textual and audio-visual prompts, helping users correct their mistakes.

As users gain confidence, they can simulate competitive scenarios using AR/VR headsets, where the AI continues to offer real-time assistance, fine tuning their skillsets .

The app eliminates the need for physical infrastructure, allowing users to train conveniently thus saving time and costs.

The 3D environment will be designed using Unreal Engine, enhancing reality in the virtualization.
